Mission

To unite humans in order to help enable a better reality for all lifeforms.

  • Terraform Venus.
  • Enable or help enable O'Neill colonies and Stanford Toruses. For the purpose of longer term human settlement, mining, scientific outposts.

  • Extract hydrocarbons from Titan. Provided no life has been found on the planet.

Values

  • Trust.
  • Help reduce and eventually eliminate suffering
